Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find my application number?

Your application number is provided immediately after successful submission of your passport application. It's also sent to your registered email address. The format is DESH followed by 11 digits.

How often is the tracking information updated?

The tracking information is updated in real-time as your application moves through different stages of processing. You'll receive email notifications for major status changes.

What if my application status shows "Under Review" for too long?

If your application has been under review for more than the expected processing time, please contact our customer support or visit the passport office where you submitted your application.

Can I track multiple applications with the same account?

Yes, if you have multiple applications, you can track each one individually using their respective application numbers. Consider creating an account for easier management.
